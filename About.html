Autobiography

My name is Maxwell Ikhide Ohiorenoya. i hails from Owan east local government.my mum's name is Doris paul Dedeaze Alemoh. she was a pretty lady whose life was cut short by death. My dad's name is Charles Afeare Ohiorenoya, from Ake , Owan East local government area. his life was also cut short by death. Both of them died at a prime age. I was take in together with my mum by my grand father after the death of my Dad. This happens to be the genesis of my life's journey.Shortly after my grand dad took us in my mum passed leaving me in the hands of my grand dad who did everything humanly possible to raise me together with my step grand mum who was pretty young at the time and raising her own kids to the best of her knowledge. I attented a Primary public school but went on to attend the second best private school at the time (Mustard Seed College, Fugar).
